Frequently Asked Qeustions and Answers

Q? When did you start playing?
A. When I was 16 years old.
Q? Is bass the only instrument you know how to play?
A. No, I also play Drums, guitar and Keyboard.
Q? How often do you practice?
A. When I first started playing, I would practice about 3 hours a day, for 5 times a week. Now, I practice about an hour 3 times a week.
Q? Who have been your go to bassplayers for inspiration?
A. Abraham laburiel, Emmanuel Espinosa, Victor Wooten, just to mention the top three influences in the way I play.
